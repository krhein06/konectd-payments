What to prepare for a review

Payments snapshot
  • Monthly volume & average ticket
  • How you accept payments today (in-person, online, invoices)
  • Any surcharging/cash-discount preferences (if applicable)
  • Current statement (optional but helpful)
Operational notes
  • Pain points (reconciliation, reporting, chargebacks, deposits)
  • Any POS/invoicing tools you use today
  • Locations and team roles (if relevant)
  • Goals (reduce admin, increase visibility, faster collections)
