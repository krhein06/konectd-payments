What to prepare for a review

Payroll snapshot
  • Employee count + pay frequency
  • Hourly vs. salary mix (rough estimate)
  • Any job-costing or department tracking needs
  • Current payroll approach/provider (optional)
Operational notes
  • Timekeeping needs (mobile, kiosk, approvals)
  • Onboarding/offboarding steps you want to simplify
  • Pain points (compliance, reporting, errors, time)
  • Goals (less admin, more accuracy, better visibility)
