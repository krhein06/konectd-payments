Our Process

Step 1

Business & Operational Review

We review your current workflows: how revenue is collected, how payroll is run, and how reporting is handled. The goal is to identify where time is lost, where errors occur, and where visibility breaks down.

Step 2

Friction & Cost Diagnosis

We identify the specific drivers of admin pain and cost: reconciliation gaps, unclear deposits, reporting limitations, payroll workflow issues, and any recurring operational “fire drills.”

Step 3

Optimization Plan

You receive a clear plan: what to change, why it matters, and how we’ll implement it. This includes improvements across payments, payroll, and reporting—plus additional business opportunities we uncover during the review.

Step 4

Implementation & Transition

We coordinate setup and transition with minimal disruption. We guide onboarding, training, and initial reporting so your team can operate confidently without a long learning curve.

Step 5

Ongoing Support & Business Access

We stay available after go-live. As your business grows, we can revisit operational structure, cash flow, and performance. You also have access to the full set of services Konectd Solutions offers—so you can solve more than one problem with one team.
